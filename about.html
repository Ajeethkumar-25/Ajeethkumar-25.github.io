My Story

With a foundation in Computer Science and a Master's in Artificial Intelligence (ongoing at Chandigarh University), I've spent the last 5+ years building technology solutions that evolve from reactive tools into proactive, autonomous systems.

My journey began in technical support at SpiceJet, where I learned the value of user-centric problem solving. I transitioned into technical consulting at Reveille Technologies, designing solution architectures for enterprise clients. Today, as an Agentic AI Engineer at Annular Technologies, I architect multi-agent platforms that think, plan, and execute autonomously.

I believe the next wave of AI isn't just about smarter models — it's about smarter orchestration. That's what I build every day.
